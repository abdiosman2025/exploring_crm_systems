# CRM Research Report

## 1. What is CRM?
Customer Relationship Management (CRM) is software that helps companies manage customer interactions, store data, and improve relationships. It centralizes customer information for sales, marketing, and support teams.

---

## 2. Why Organizations Use CRM
Organizations use CRM systems for:
- Sales tracking
- Customer management
- Marketing automation
- Customer support
- Reporting and analytics

---

## 3. Business Problems CRM Solves
- Retail: tracks customer purchases
- Healthcare: manages patient communication
- Education: tracks student engagement
- Manufacturing: manages client relationships
- Nonprofits: tracks donors and fundraising

---

## 4. Major CRM Modules
- Contacts
- Accounts
- Leads
- Opportunities
- Activities
- Tasks
- Reports
- Dashboards

---

## 5. Commercial CRM Comparison

- Salesforce: powerful but expensive
- HubSpot: easy to use, free version available
- Zoho CRM: affordable and flexible
- Microsoft Dynamics 365: strong enterprise integration

---

## 6. Open Source CRM Comparison

- SuiteCRM: powerful and mature
- EspoCRM: simple and easy to use
- Odoo CRM: full business suite

---

## 7. Open Source CRM Evaluation (EspoCRM)
EspoCRM was selected for evaluation.

It includes:
- Contacts module
- Leads module
- Dashboard
- Activities tracking

It is easy to use and suitable for small to medium businesses.

---
